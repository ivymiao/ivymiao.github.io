A Hot Air Balloon Paper Model ( •̀∀•́ )

Hi, this is a hot air balloon paper model made in Pepakura Designer, a software which features to automatically unfold 3D polygon-mesh models.

At the time I was in the publicity department in Student Union of Electronic Engineering, I designed this paper model for Student Festival in our department. After building a hot air balloon 3D model, I flattened the surfaces to get the surface development. You can cut the paper along lines and fold the paper, to achieve this hot air balloon paper model!
